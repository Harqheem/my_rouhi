Apology to my Bighead 🥺

Hmmmm... here I am, apologizing for subtly dismissing my baby's feelings about wanting to lock lips with me. And here is me saying lo siento, désolée, mianhaeyo, yi haƙuri, ma binu si mi Olori mi.

I truly did not mean to dismiss your feelings that way, and I promise it won't happen again, Mi Amor. You know that spending time and talking with you for hours is one of the things I enjoy the most, and it would not be traded for anything except perhaps the intimate moments we'll be having soon in the nearest future. 😏

I want to wake up every morning with you wrapped in my arms. Soft smiles, gentle kisses, hair stroking all of this after having several matches during the night. 🌙

I pray the Almighty preserves us and allows us to keep growing in love, because you, Mardiyyah, are the only one I want to love in this Dunya and Akhirah.

Thank you for not giving up on me. I will always strive to be a better man you'll forever be proud of.

I love you so much, Oyinkanosla mi. 😘

A Du'a for Us

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِي حُبِّنَا وَاجْمَعْنَا عَلَى خَيْرٍ

"O Allah, bless us in our love and unite us upon goodness."

I pray the Almighty preserves us — in this Dunya and in Akhirah. You, Mardiyyah, are the only one I want to love in both worlds.

وَجَعَلَ بَيْنَكُم مَّوَدَّةً وَرَحْمَةً

"He placed between you affection and mercy." — Qur'an 30:21

May this love continue to grow, may we always find our way back to each other, and may Allah make us a sign of His mercy for one another. Āmīn.
